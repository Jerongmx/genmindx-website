GenX — Our Machine Vision Software

GenX is a high-performance C#/.NET machine vision framework designed for industrial cameras, Halcon algorithms and optional deep-learning models. It focuses on low-latency image pipelines and easy system integration.

  • Multi-camera support (GigE, USB3, GenTL)
  • Halcon 24.x integrations and optional YOLO models
  • Real-time IO, TCP/IP and Modbus support
  • Dashboard, logs, and configurable alarm actions
